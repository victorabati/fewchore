CUSTOMER LOAN

We provide quick and accesible consumer and business loans to individuals and SMEs.

FUNDS MANAGEMENT

ASSET FINANCE

We are quick in making decision to Finance the acquisition of assets. We offer both finance and operating lease options.

ISSUING OF VENDORS COUPONS, CARDS, AND TOKENS

We issue coupon cards, token stamps, and vouchers for purchase (and repayment) of goods/services from select vendors

LOCAL AND INTERNATIONAL TRADE FINANCE

We provide contractors and vendors with the financial support to execute local purchase orders (LPOs) and work orders for their client companies

DEBT FACTORING

We provide the refinancing of debts and receivables from clients at a discount

DEBT SECURITIZATION

We Identify pools of contractual debt/receivables and transform them into marketable securities such as bonds through suitable repackaging of cash-flows that they generate

FINANCIAL CONSULTANCY

We provide our clients with professional advisory services towards better wealth planning and funds management

LOAN SYNDICATION

We partner with other financial companies and institutions to lend on projects in order to manage our risk exposure level

WAREHOUSE-RECEIPT FINANCE

We provide loans to manufacturers and processors with their goods and commodities to be held in our trust as collateral.
